What is HelaLearn?

HelaLearn is Sri Lanka's smart AI learning assistant built for students and developers. It helps you scan question papers, understand answers, learn concepts, and even generate or debug code. Whether you're studying or building something new, HelaLearn guides you step-by-step.

How Credits Work

Credits are used to interact with HelaLearn's AI features. Each question, scan, or message uses a small number of credits depending on complexity. You can reload credits anytime through secure payment options. Your unused credits never expire.

Privacy & Safety

Your privacy is protected with end-to-end security. Question paper scans, code, and conversations are processed safely and not stored after your session. We never share your data with third parties. Everything you create with HelaLearn remains fully yours.
