Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on our homepage. You can use your email address or sign up with Google for faster access. After registration, you'll receive 50 free credits to get started.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, Hela Learn is available as a web application that works on all devices. You can access it from your mobile browser, and it's optimized for mobile use. We're working on native mobile apps for iOS and Android.

How do credits work?

Credits are used to interact with Hela Learn's AI assistant. Each message you send consumes credits based on complexity. Simple questions use 1 credit, while code generation or complex queries may use 2-5 credits. You get 50 free credits monthly, and can purchase additional credits if needed.

How can I purchase more credits?

You can purchase credits by clicking the "Buy Credits" button in the top navigation. We offer various packages starting from 100 credits. Payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

The AI is not responding. What should I do?

If Hela Learn isn't responding, try these steps:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Ensure you have sufficient credits
If the issue persists, contact our support team.

Can I use Hela Learn for commercial projects?

Yes, you can use code generated by Hela Learn in commercial projects. However, we recommend reviewing and testing all code before implementation. We're not liable for any issues that may arise from using generated code in production environments.

Is my code private and secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your code and conversations are encrypted and not stored on our servers after your session ends. We don't use your data to train our models or share it with third parties.

What data do you collect?

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our service: account information, usage statistics, and payment details (handled by secure third-party providers). We don't read or store your chat messages or code after processing.
